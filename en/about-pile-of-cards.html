--- title: "How to play Pile of Cards" layout: en_pile-of-cards description: "Learn about Pile of Cards - a fast paced Solitaire game for Windows, available for free for Android (Google Play), Windows (Microsoft Store) and Tizen." ---

Start with an huge pile of cards.


Find the first King.


Then find a Queen.


Continue getting the next lower card until the Ace.


Then start again with a King.


Continue until the final Ace.


After the final Ace, you win.
