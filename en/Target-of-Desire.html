Target of Desire


Target of Desire follows 2 characters: Maia and Maria.

Maia exists in the video game.

Maria exists on social media under the username "I am named Maria."

The audience experiences both of them.

The challenge of Target of Desire is to discover the reality that Maia is experiencing, the reality that Maria is experiencing, the reality that the audience is experiencing, and how they relate to each other.
