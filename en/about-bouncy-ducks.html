--- title: "How to play Bouncy Ducks" layout: en_bouncy-ducks description: "Learn about Bouncy Ducks - an addicting action game, available for free for Android (Google Play), Windows (Microsoft Store) and Tizen." ---

There are flying ducks

Tap the screen to throw a ball - get points for each duck hit by a ball.

You get points when blocks are knocked over.

Some ducks bounce.

When you get enough points, you win the level.
